Szrot is a racing game about the underappreciated cars and cultures of Eastern Europe.

* Szrot in Polish (or Å rot in Czech, Slovak and Serbo-croatian) is pronounced "sh-rot" and is a term for junkyards that sell used car parts. It is also used to refer to old beater cars. The term may be used in a negative way, but we use it endearingly.
